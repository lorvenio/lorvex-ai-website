## Overview

Agentic AI shifts the focus from static models to autonomous systems that can plan, act, and adapt. These systems coordinate tools, context, and multi-agent collaboration to deliver end-to-end outcomes.

## Why it matters

- Higher throughput for knowledge workflows
- Reduced operational latency in decision cycles
- Improved resilience through multi-agent redundancy

## What to build first

Start with bounded workflows that have clear success criteria. Focus on observability, agent evaluation, and human-in-the-loop escalation before expanding autonomy.
